What Is Tenarse?
Our project aims to contribute to the programmers' world with a social network through a mobile app and a webpage. Moreover, with this social network, we want to encourage the interest of programmers to learn and share knowledge, therefore, enhance their passion and skills in this area.

We are also building this platform to make companies aware of the skills that programmers demonstrate on our social network before hiring them. In this report, you can see the development and creation plan step by step of TENARSE.
